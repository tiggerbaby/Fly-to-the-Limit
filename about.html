About Us

FTTL is owned by a husband and wife team, Olga and Bruce McKenzie. Both born in Southland, travelled extensively, both have a commercial pilot's licenses with 19 and 9 years respectively flying for Middle Eastern airlines. We offering adventure flights,transport and sightseeing based in the scenic capital of New Zealand, therefore the world. Operating out of Queenstown and Wanaka, FTTL offer tourists a range of scenic flights in both fixed wing and helicopters, as well as high adventure in a stunt plane, or the most peaceful flight experience possible in a glider high above the mountains. They specialize in one-off charters, and delivery and pickup for trampers, also providing a provisioning service for fresh supplies during long excursions. We own and operate a Cessna Caravan Amphibian which can take up to 7 people to any remote lake in the area. We also own a DG Flugzeugbau DG-1001S with a Cessna Turbo Stationair to tow it. The Cessna is also available for custom scenic tours.
